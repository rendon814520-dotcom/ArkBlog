Capstone Overview

Problems Aimed to Resolve

The digital marginalization of visually impaired individuals remains a significant challenge, as most applications rely heavily on visual interfaces, limiting access to educational and recreational content. This project, EchoVenture, was conceived to address this gap by developing an immersive, audio-based application. The primary objective was to create a navigable virtual experience of Davao City's landmarks, specifically the People's Park and Philippine Eagle Sanctuary, using 3D binaural audio and text-to-speech narration to foster independence and cultural awareness among visually impaired users.

Technologies Used

Unity Engine, C#, Visual Studio Code, ChatGPT, and Gemini AI.

Main Features of the System

Immersive audio navigation, Binaural audio cues, Text-to-speech narration, Interactive quizzes, and User-friendly interface.
